Relief Operations Updates

Stay informed about our progress and ongoing relief efforts

November 11, 2025

Website Launch & Donation Platform

We are excited to announce the launch of our official donation website. This platform allows donors to contribute securely and track the impact of their donations in real-time.

  • Secure online donation system now live
  • Real-time donation tracking and transparency
  • User profiles to manage contributions
November 10, 2025

1,200 Food Packs Distributed

Our team successfully distributed 1,200 food packs to families in the hardest-hit areas of Cebu City, including Barangays Guadalupe, Lahug, and Mabolo.

What was included: Rice, canned goods, instant noodles, coffee, sugar, and cooking oil.

November 9, 2025

Medical Mission Completed

Free medical consultation and medicines were provided to 450 individuals in evacuation centers. Our volunteer doctors and nurses treated various typhoon-related health issues.

  • 450 patients treated
  • Free medicines distributed
  • Basic health screening conducted
November 8, 2025

Emergency Response Activated

Immediately after Typhoon Tino made landfall, our emergency response team was deployed to assess damage and begin coordinating relief operations.

Initial assessment: Over 2,000 families affected, 500+ homes damaged or destroyed.

November 7, 2025

Pre-Typhoon Preparations

Evacuation centers were prepared and emergency supplies were stockpiled as Typhoon Tino approached Cebu.
