About Typhoon Tino Relief

Our Story

On November 8, 2025, Typhoon Tino made landfall in Cebu, Philippines, bringing devastating winds and torrential rains that affected thousands of families. In response to this disaster, the Typhoon Tino Relief initiative was created to coordinate aid and support for affected communities.

Our Mission

We are dedicated to providing immediate disaster relief and long-term recovery support to families and communities affected by Typhoon Tino. Our goal is to ensure that every family has access to food, water, shelter, and medical care while we work together to rebuild stronger communities.

What We Do

  • Emergency Relief: Distribution of food, water, and emergency supplies
  • Shelter Support: Providing temporary housing and rebuilding materials
  • Medical Assistance: Mobile health clinics and medical supply distribution
  • Livelihood Recovery: Supporting families in rebuilding their sources of income
  • Community Rebuilding: Infrastructure repair and community development

About the Creator

Website Developer: TechLA Corporation

Purpose: This platform was created to streamline donation management and provide transparency in relief operations for Typhoon Tino victims in Cebu.

Technology: Built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, powered by Supabase database to ensure secure and efficient donation tracking.

Mission: To leverage technology in serving our kababayans during times of crisis and facilitate community-driven disaster response.

Transparency & Accountability

We believe in full transparency. All donations are tracked and recorded in our database, and regular updates are provided to donors about how their contributions are being used to help typhoon victims.
