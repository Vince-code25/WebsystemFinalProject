Our Mission

Typhoon Tino has left thousands of families in Cebu without homes, food, and basic necessities. Our mission is to provide immediate relief and long-term support to help communities rebuild and recover.

Urgent Needs

  • Clean drinking water and water purification systems
  • Food supplies and emergency rations
  • Temporary shelter materials and tents
  • Medical supplies and first aid kits
  • Clothing and blankets
  • Hygiene kits and sanitation supplies

How You Can Help

Every contribution makes a difference. Whether it's a monetary donation, goods, or volunteer services, your support helps families rebuild their lives.
